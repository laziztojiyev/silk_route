{% blocktrans %} SILK ROUTE DMC (Destination Management Company) welcomes you to an extraordinary journey through the East, delving deep into traditions and histories spanning thousands of years. Our professional team guarantees high-quality service and aims to enhance your understanding of our culture, history, and geography. We offer a wide range of services, including:
- Guided tours to the cities of the Great Silk Road: Tashkent, Samarkand, Bukhara, Khiva, and other historical sites.
- Visa support.
- Hotel booking with various levels of comfort.
- Rental services for vehicles of different classes.
- Booking of air and train tickets.
- Arrangements for children's and student groups.
- Participation in Uzbekistan's various festivals.

Our guides and translators boast deep knowledge of the region's history, as well as the culture and traditions of the local populace.
